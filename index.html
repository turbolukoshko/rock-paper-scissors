Rock, Paper, Scissors

Welcome to the classic game of Rock, Paper, Scissors! This simple yet entertaining game is a test of strategy and quick decision-making.

Rules:

  1. Rock crushes Scissors: If you choose Rock and your opponent chooses Scissors, you win.
  2. Scissors cuts Paper: If you choose Scissors and your opponent chooses Paper, you win.
  3. Paper covers Rock: If you choose Paper and your opponent chooses Rock, you win.
  4. The game is a tie if both players choose the same move.

How to Play:

  1. Click on one of the buttons below to make your move: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  2. The computer will randomly choose its move.
  3. The winner will be determined based on the rules mentioned above.
  4. The game consists of 3 rounds. The player with the most round victories is the overall winner.

Have fun playing and may the best player win!
